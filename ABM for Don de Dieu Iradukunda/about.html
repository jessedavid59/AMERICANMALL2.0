Story of the mall

The story of the mall revolves around this concept: to import
the authentic, dreamy and iconic American atmosphere and fashion,
and make it accessible through our shop, as indicated in home page
description on our website.

Vision

American-Burundi Mall is a groundbreaking project aimed at boosting the
local economy and strengthening ties between Burundi and the United States.
We are bringing a modern shopping experience and a significant positive impact.
